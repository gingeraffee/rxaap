ERC Update: Next Steps

Posted: January 15, 2026

The ERC reviewed emergency readiness and is implementing improvements in drill execution, communication reliability, and roll-call accountability procedures.

What’s happening next

  • Intruder/safety drill will be scheduled and dated at the next ERC meeting.
  • Room-specific evacuation maps will be compiled, approved, laminated, and posted.
  • Leadership expectations for radios and roll call will be communicated via BambooHR.

Reminder

During emergencies, follow ERC Officer-in-Charge direction and move to assigned safety zones when instructed.
