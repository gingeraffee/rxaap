Purpose

The ERC supports employee safety by coordinating emergency response readiness, safety trainings, policy awareness, and hazard reporting. During emergency events, follow ERC direction and move to assigned safety zones when instructed.

Reporting Guidance

See something? Say something. Report safety concerns immediately.

  • In-person: Notify an ERC member or the OIC.
  • Urgent hazards: Report immediately—do not wait.
  • What to report: unsafe conditions, hazards, near-misses, suspicious activity, or emergency concerns.
  • What happens next: ERC evaluates, escalates as needed, and documents follow-up actions.
