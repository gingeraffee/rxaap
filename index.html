Purpose

The Employee Relations Committee (ERC) helps keep our workplace safe by developing and sharing safety policies, training, and preparedness guidance throughout the year. Employees are encouraged to report safety concerns, hazards, near-misses, or suggestions to any ERC member at any time.

For emergency readiness, each ERC member is assigned to lead a designated Safety Zone and help guide employees during an incident. If you’re unsure which Safety Zone you’re assigned to, ask any ERC member or your supervisor.

During any emergency, the ERC Leader is the primary on-site safety lead and is responsible for directing the response. The ERC Leader receives monthly emergency preparedness training and will follow established procedures to help ensure everyone’s safety.
