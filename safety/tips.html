How to use this page

Add new tips as cards below. Keep them short, specific, and action-focused. (Example: “Do X” beats “Be careful.”)

Severe Weather: Move Immediately

When directed, go to your assigned safety zone right away. Don’t stop to gather items.

Category: Severe Weather

Roll Call Readiness

Managers should be ready to account for their teams during emergency roll call.

Category: Accountability

Radio Check

If radios are in use, confirm you can receive messages at the start of your shift.

Category: Communication

Know Your Zone

If you don’t know your assigned safety zone, ask any ERC member.

Category: Preparedness
