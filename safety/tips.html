Safety Tips

In an emergency event, being prepared can be the difference between life or death. Keep these safety tips in mind!

Severe Weather: Move Immediately

When the ERC sounds the alarm, go to your assigned safety zone right away. Don’t stop to gather items.

Roll Call Readiness

ERC Leaders are required to account for all employees in an assigned safety zone by calling roll. The Safety Zone Leader will report any missing staff to the ERC OIC.

Radio Check

If you are assigned a radio, always keep it charged and set to channel 8. This is the fastest way to communicate in an emergency situation.

Know Your Zone

If you don’t know your assigned safety zone, ask any ERC member.

Stop, Look, Listen

Before walking into an aisle or doorway in the warehouse, Stop, Look & Listen for pallet jacks, forklifts or carts. Don't trust that the person operating the equipment sees you and stay out of the path whenever possible.
