Training Modules

These are short refreshers. See the complete procedures so you're always ready in an emergency.

Badge Access: No Tailgating, No “Hold the Door”

Only authorized employees may enter—everyone must badge in individually.

  • Do not open or hold the door for anyone, even if you recognize them.
  • Every person must use their own badge to enter the building or warehouse.
  • If someone cannot access the door, direct them to badge in or contact their supervisor/security for access.

Evacuation Basics: Move Fast, Use the Closest Exit

In an evacuation, seconds matter—know where you’re going before you need to go.

  • Evacuate immediately when instructed—do not delay to gather personal items.
  • Always know your closest exit (and an alternate route if it’s blocked).
  • Review building evacuation maps posted around the facility to confirm routes and assembly areas.

Emergency Roles: Follow ERC Direction

ERC members train monthly and coordinate response—your job is to follow instructions and stay calm.

  • During an emergency, follow directions from ERC members and your assigned Safety Zone lead.
  • Trust that the ERC is monitoring conditions (including weather) and prioritizing everyone’s safety.
  • Do not self-direct movement between areas—wait for the all-clear or next instruction.
