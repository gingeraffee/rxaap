Training Modules

These are short refreshers. Keep modules focused on “what to do” and “who does it.”

Roll Call Procedure (Managers)

What managers must do during emergency accountability checks.

  • Know your expected headcount.
  • Report missing/unaccounted employees immediately to the ERC.
  • Follow ERC OIC direction and do not self-direct movement between zones.

Audience: Managers

Radio Expectations

How to keep emergency comms clear and useful.

  • Keep messages short and plain-language.
  • Do not interrupt active emergency instructions.
  • If radio fails, follow the posted fallback procedure.

Audience: Leaders / ERC

Severe Weather Response Basics

The “do this, not that” fundamentals.

  • Move to your assigned safety zone when directed.
  • Do not leave your zone until cleared by ERC OIC.
  • Help keep aisles/paths clear during movement.

Audience: All Employees
