Available Trainings

Use these as refresher materials. Add more modules over time.

Roll Call Procedure (Managers)

Know how roll call is taken during emergencies and what managers must report.

  • Bring your roster (or know your headcount)
  • Report missing persons immediately to ERC
  • Follow ERC Officer-in-Charge direction

Radio Expectations

When radios are in use, keep traffic short, clear, and emergency-focused.

  • Confirm you can receive messages at shift start
  • Use plain language (no codes)
  • Don’t interrupt critical announcements
